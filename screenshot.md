## App Icon – Nano Banana 2 Prompt

> Direkt verwendbar für Android (1024×1024 px) und iOS (1024×1024 px).  
> Muss bei 48×48 dp (Homescreen) genauso lesbar sein wie bei 512×512 px (Play Store Listing).

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
```
Create a professional mobile app icon for a medical document scanning app called "OneScan".

Output: 1024×1024 px square, PNG, no transparency, no rounded corners (the OS applies them).

Design concept:
- Background: solid deep blue #1a5c9a filling the entire canvas. No gradients, no noise — flat, clean.
- Centered graphic element: a stylized white document (portrait A4 ratio, slightly tilted 8° clockwise) with:
  - Clean white fill (#ffffff)
  - Subtle light-grey horizontal lines on the document suggesting printed text (4–5 lines, color #dbe8f6, 3px height each, evenly spaced in the lower 60% of the document shape)
  - Rounded corners on the document: 12px
  - The document takes up roughly 55% of the icon width
- Overlapping the top-right corner of the document: a circular scanner / camera lens badge:
  - Circle diameter: ~28% of icon width
  - Outer ring: white, 5px stroke
  - Inner fill: #0d6ebd (slightly darker blue than background)
  - Inside the circle: a simple white scan/viewfinder symbol — two pairs of corner brackets (like ⌜⌟) in white, 3px stroke, no fill
- No text, no letters, no numbers anywhere on the icon.
- No drop shadows. No glows. No gradients.
- Clean, flat design — professional healthcare aesthetic.
- The overall feel: trustworthy, clinical, modern — similar to medical software UI (CGM MAXX style).

Color palette (strict):
- Background: #1a5c9a
- Document: #ffffff
- Document lines: #dbe8f6
- Scanner circle fill: #0d6ebd
- All icon strokes and symbols: #ffffff
```

**Adaptive Icon (Android) – zusätzlicher Hinweis:**

Das exportierte 1024×1024 PNG direkt als `foregroundImage` in `app.json` verwenden. Der bedeutsame Inhalt (Dokument + Scanner-Badge) muss innerhalb der zentralen **72dp Safe Zone** liegen — d.h. ca. in den inneren 720×720 px des 1024er Canvas. Hintergrundfarbe in `app.json`:

```json
"adaptiveIcon": {
  "foregroundImage": "./assets/icon.png",
  "backgroundColor": "#1a5c9a"
}
```
